📖 System Guide

🔧 Quick Setup

Step 1: Set Your School Details

Find the SCHOOL_CONFIG object at the top of the JavaScript section. Edit name, motto, address, session, and logo URLs to match your school.

Step 2: Connect Your Google Sheets

Find the CLASS_LINKS object. For each class, paste your published Google Sheet CSV URLs for first, second, and third terms.

Step 3: Set Login Credentials

Find CREDENTIALS and change admin.username, admin.password, and student.password to your preferred values.

📊 Google Sheet Format

Required Columns

Your sheet needs: Name, Admission No, Gender, Class Type, Class Section, Term. Subject columns follow the pattern: SubjectName_CA1, SubjectName_CA2, SubjectName_Exams.

Publish as CSV

In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web → Select sheet → CSV → Publish. Copy the URL and paste into CLASS_LINKS.

🎨 Customization

Class-Specific Report Card Colors

Find the section labeled "REPORT CARD — CLASS-SPECIFIC COLOURS". The primary class category can have its own color theme.

School Name, Motto, Address

Find the SCHOOL_CONFIG object and edit: name, motto, address, session, resumption.

🏆 Awards & Certificates

Best Student Awards (Automatic)

Go to Analytics → Load All Analytics first. Then go to Awards → Best Student Award and click Generate. Certificates are produced automatically for all primary classes.

Certificate Signatures

Upload signature images to PostImage and paste direct links into SCHOOL_CONFIG: headmasterSig, primaryFormMasterSig.

Testimonial Certificates (Automatic)

Go to Awards → Testimonial Certificate and click Generate. It pulls every Primary 6 student (all arms) from their class sheet and produces one testimonial per student, with the Head Teacher's signature and school stamp applied automatically — the student's own signature line is left blank for them to sign.

🔐 Student Term PIN Codes

Term Lock PINs (Student Dashboard)

Each term in the student result portal is locked with a 4-digit PIN. Share these with students or parents when a term is ready for viewing.

  • 📘 First Term PIN: 4545
  • 📗 Second Term PIN: 8787
  • 📙 Third Term PIN: 9191

To change these PINs, locate the TERM_PINS object in the JavaScript section near the bottom of the file.

🌐 Deploying on Netlify

Step-by-Step Deployment

  • Go to netlify.com and sign up / log in
  • Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
  • Drag and drop your index.html file into the upload box
  • Netlify gives you a public URL immediately
  • To update: go to your site dashboard → Deploys → drag new file
